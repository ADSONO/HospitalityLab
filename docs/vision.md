# Vision

## What is HospitalityLab?

HospitalityLab is a personal laboratory project focused on exploring, understanding and documenting the technologies behind modern hospitality businesses.

The project covers a wide range of topics including property management systems, booking platforms, networking, television and satellite distribution, electrical infrastructure, automation and operational workflows.

## Why?

The goal is not to build a real hotel or camping business.

The goal is to understand how all these systems work together, experiment with them, document them and learn from the process.

Most importantly, HospitalityLab is an excuse to explore technologies that I find interesting, whether they are modern, legacy, simple or unnecessarily complex.

## Philosophy

Because building a website and playing with a network switch wasn't enough.

## Principles

- Learn by building
- Document everything
- Experiment freely
- Accept mistakes
- Preserve knowledge
- Have fun

## Long-Term Goal

Create a comprehensive knowledge base and laboratory environment covering as many aspects of hospitality technology and infrastructure as possible.

Not because it is necessary.

Simply because it is interesting.
