# Current Automation System

## Overview

This document describes the current automation equipment available within HospitalityLab.

The automation part of the project focuses on industrial control systems, building automation and infrastructure management concepts commonly found in commercial and hospitality environments.

## Current Equipment

### Schneider Electric Modicon M221

Compact PLC used for automation experiments, control logic development and infrastructure testing.

### Siemens SIMATIC S7-1200

Industrial PLC platform intended for more advanced automation and control system experiments.

## Areas of Interest

- Lighting Control
- Energy Management
- Technical Monitoring
- Building Automation
- HVAC Concepts
- Alarm Management
- Infrastructure Control
- And more...

## Purpose

The objective is not to reproduce a complete industrial installation.

Instead, the goal is to understand, experiment with and document automation technologies on a smaller scale.

## Current Status

🚧 Early Stage

The hardware is available and operational.

The overall automation architecture is still being defined and will evolve as HospitalityLab grows.
