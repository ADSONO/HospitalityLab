About

A personal lab for learning, testing and documenting.

HospitalityLab started as a slightly unreasonable idea: recreate and document the technical ecosystem of a hotel, camping and restaurant environment from a garage lab.

The goal is not to build a production-ready hospitality business. The goal is to learn, experiment, repair, configure, break things, understand them and document the process.

Based in France

Unless otherwise specified, the project follows European and French technical context: DVB television standards, 230/400 V three-phase electrical systems and local infrastructure practices.

Project areas

One project, many systems.

📡

TV & Satellite

Terrestrial TV, Astra reception, coaxial distribution, FRPRO EVO HD, Televes equipment and satellite experiments.

🌐

Networking

MikroTik, Ubiquiti, Fortinet, fiber distribution, VLAN planning and future guest/infrastructure networks.

⚡

Electrical

Small-scale power distribution cabinet, second-hand equipment, 230/400 V three-phase concepts and safety-first experimentation.

🤖

Automation

Schneider M221, Siemens S7-1200, PLC experiments, building automation ideas and infrastructure control.

💻

Software

Frontend, backend, API development, public website, reservation system and staff tools.

🏨

Hospitality Systems

Booking, PMS concepts, operations, guest workflows and the glue between physical infrastructure and software.
