Infrastructure

Current Systems

A living overview of the physical and software systems currently being explored inside HospitalityLab.

TV & Satellite

Terrestrial and satellite distribution.

The TV setup currently includes a small terrestrial antenna, Johansson 7328 mast amplifier, FRPRO EVO HD programmable amplifier and a 4-way RF splitter. The satellite side is based around a 100 cm dish, quattro LNB, Televes NevoSwitch and Televes T.0X modules.

Current status Operational / Experimental

Network

Routing, switching and VLAN planning.

Current equipment includes MikroTik, Ubiquiti UniFi, Ubiquiti Edge, Fortinet and fiber distribution gear. The main router decision is still open between a FortiGate 60F and a MikroTik RB5009.

Current status Planning

Electrical

Small-scale power cabinet.

The electrical cabinet is based on second-hand equipment and is currently under construction. The project is based in France and follows a European 230/400 V three-phase context.

Current status Under construction

Automation

PLC-based automation experiments.

Automation experiments are planned around a Schneider Modicon M221 and a Siemens S7-1200, with future use cases around lighting, monitoring, alarms and building automation.

Current status Early stage
