# Current Network

## Overview

The network infrastructure of HospitalityLab is still evolving and several design decisions remain open.

The objective is to build a flexible network architecture capable of supporting management systems, television infrastructure, servers, automation and future hospitality-related services.

## Available Equipment

### Routing & Security

- MikroTik RB5009
- Fortinet FortiGate 60F

Both platforms are currently being considered for the role of primary gateway and firewall.

### Switching

- Ubiquiti EdgeSwitch 12 Fiber
- Ubiquiti EdgeSwitch 24 Lite

The EdgeSwitch 12 Fiber is expected to serve as the core fiber distribution platform.

The EdgeSwitch 24 Lite currently represents the most likely candidate for copper connectivity within the main building.

### Additional Equipment

- Various MikroTik devices
- Ubiquiti UniFi equipment
- Additional Ubiquiti Edge devices

## Current Questions

### Main Router

The primary question currently being explored is whether the network should be built around:

- FortiGate 60F
- MikroTik RB5009

Both options have advantages and will likely require further testing before a final decision is made.

### Network Architecture

The final network topology is still under development.

Several aspects remain undefined, including:

- VLAN strategy
- Fiber distribution
- Guest access
- Management networks
- Future expansion

## Status

🚧 Work in Progress

The hardware foundation already exists, but the overall architecture is still being designed and refined.
